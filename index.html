How long does a typical outpatient treatment program last?
The duration of outpatient treatment at Strive Recovery Fort Wayne depends on your individual needs and goals. What can I expect during my first appointment at Strive Recovery Fort Wayne?
Your first appointment will be a comprehensive assessment with one of our licensed counselors.
